Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?

To create an event, navigate to the 'Host' tab and tap the '+' button. Follow the prompts to add details like event name, date, time, location, and description. You can also set ticket prices and availability.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and popular digital wallets. All transactions are securely processed through our payment provider.

How do I report an issue?

You can report an issue directly from an event page or contact our support team via the options below. Please provide as much detail as possible to help us resolve it quickly.
